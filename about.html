About the Author


I’m Olivia Wyzga, an aspiring software engineer and current high schooler and Seattle-area resident. This website combines two of my passions, coding and the environment. Along with this, I’m an avid athlete and scholar. As a student past enrolled in AP Environmental Science, the course opened my eyes to issues I did not know existed. I created this website by hand and I hope it informed you more about the issues we face and gave you ideas about what you can do to help.
